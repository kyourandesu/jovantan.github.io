My experiences from Primary school to now

2017

In 2017, I was a primary 6 student of Ang Mo Kio Primary School, studying for my first national exam, the Primary School Leaving Examination (PSLE). After countless hours of studying and revising, I have gotten a raw score of 241 for my PSLE.

2018

In 2018, I got into Maris Stella High School (MSHS) and thus begun my secondary school journey. Since I was in band since Primary 1, I decided joined Symphonic Band as my Co-Curricular Activity (CCA) again in Seconadry School. Also, I played the trumpet in band.

2019

I was Secondary 2 in 2019, studying for my streaming examinations, hoping meet the high requirements to get the subject combinations I want. Other than that, I also spent alot of time practicing my instrument as my school band will be having a big performance at the Esplanade!

2020

I got my desired subject combinations! They were A Math, E math, English, Higher chinese, Pure Physics, Pure Chemistry, Computing, Elective Geography and Social studies. However, Covid 19 became a pandemic and had to go through Home Based Learning (HBL). As I did not pay much attention during HBL, I got a score of 34 for my L1R5 :(

2021

Secondary 4. O-level year. I dropped Higher chinese and took up express chinese to focus on my poor results. I studied hard, staying back to ask teacher questions and even took up 6 tuitions that year! It was also my 10th and last year being in a band! The trumpet, playing songs with my friends and performing was already part of my life so it was a little bit hard for me when it was time for me to step down. After stepping down, I could finally solely focus on Studying for my O-levels.

2022 (Before Poly)

I got a score of 8 for my L1R4! It was such a big improvement from my poor results in Sec 3 even my parents could not believe it. The hardwork really paid off and I got into the course I wanted in Singapore Polytechnic - Applied Artificial Intelligence and Analytics

Now (In Polytechnic)

Moving on from a Secondary school to a Polytechnic has not been easy. The environment, teaching methods and timetable were hard to get used to at first but with the help of my friends and teachers, I managed to adapt to it. My classmates also came from many different backgrounds but we managed to get along quite well!

Diploma in Applied AI and Analytics
(DAAA - S30)
Course Highlights

BE PART OF THE REVOLUTION: AI & data analytics are the key under-pinning technologies for transforming every industry, including manufacturing, healthcare, and more!

EMPOWER YOUR FUTURE: Gain a competitive edge pursuing your passion as a data analyst, data engineer or an AI practitioner. NEVER BE BORED: Face new challenges and projects closely related to solving real-world problems.

PROFESSIONAL CERTIFICATIONS: You can take up professional certificates from companies such as Microsoft and AI Singapore during your course of study to gain industry recognition.

INDUSTRY NOW CURRICULUM (INC): Industry Project Learning Approach - Project INC pathway. If you thrive on learning-by-doing, you can opt for this alternative learning pathway in your second and third year of study. In lieu of attending traditional module classes, you get to work as software developers at an industry-facing student agency known as Project INC on real client industry projects to gain credits.

STATE OF THE ART AI FACILITIES: The course has specially designed AI computer labs which provide students with high performing computers. The lab is also equipped with AI Deep Learning servers capable of handling heavy lifting machine learning task.
